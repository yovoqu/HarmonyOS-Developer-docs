# DevEco Studio安装HAP时报错“FileTransfer Failed: [Fail]Transfer Stop at:XXX, Reason: no space left on device”

更新时间：2026-03-10 06:16:35

来源：https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-faqs/faqs-app-debugging-62

**问题现象**
 
DevEco Studio安装HAP时报错“FileTransfer Failed: [Fail]Transfer Stop at:XXX, Reason: no space left on device”。
 

![](assets/DevEco%20Studio安装HAP时报错“FileTransfer%20Failed／%20[Fail]Transfer%20Stop%20at／XXX,%20Reason／%20no%20space%20left%20on%20device”/file-20260515130324557-0.png)

 
**解决措施**
 
设备磁盘空间不足，无法写入数据。请检查设备磁盘空间。如果是模拟器，可以点击“Wipe User Data”清除模拟器数据，然后重新运行模拟器。
