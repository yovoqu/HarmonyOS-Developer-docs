来源：https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-guides/scenario-fusion-faq-1

**现象描述**

 日志报错示例：


```text
login fail, error: {"code":1001502014,"message":"The app does not have the required scopes or permissions."}
```
